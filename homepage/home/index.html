Vorkless is a user-friendly interactive environment where labours as well as clients both are benefited by eliminating the middlemen i.e. contractors. Labours get an opportunity to display themselves on portal so as to get easily hired by clients and get fare wages. Clients on the other hand are benefited by receiving service with best of the labours around the city. We aim to create unemployment-free nation by providing equal employment opportunity to every single unemployed person
